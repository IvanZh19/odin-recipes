Mashed Potatoes

What Is It?

Mashed potatoes, or some variant of them, is a popular recipe all around the world. As the name suggests, this dish conists of potatoes mushed and seasoned until wonderfully satiating. Here, we'll teach you how you can create your own wonderful mashed potatoes at home! This recipe is meant to serve 3 people, but feel free to adjust for as big of a crowd as you need.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 russet potatoes
  • 3 tbsp butter (salted and unsalted both work)
  • 1 cup milk or half-and-half
  • 5 pieces of garlic
  • seasonings: salt, pepper, rosemary, thyme

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water up to a boil.
  2. Cut the potatoes into relatively small shapes and boil them.
  3. Melt the butter in the milk with a method of your choice.
  4. Add the garlic and seasonings to this mixture.
  5. Remove the water once they are fork-tender.
  6. Place the potatoes back on the heat to cook out excess steam.
  7. Mash the potatoes and slowly mix in the butter and milk mixture, being careful to not over-mix.
  8. Enjoy!
