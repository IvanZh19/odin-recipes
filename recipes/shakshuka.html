Shakshuka

What Is It?

Shakshuka is a simple dish of poached eggs in a tomato and bell pepper sauce. It is a popular and common dish throughout parts of North Africa and the Middle East. Similar to Turkish menemen, this dish contains many spices and simple cooking techniques that bring the flavors to life.

In this recipe, we'll teach you how you can make your own shakshuka at home! This recipe is intended to serve 2-3 people, but feel free to adjust how you'd like, and pick the flavors that you find most tasty!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1/2 green bell pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil (any kind)
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • red paprika, to taste
  • garlic (optional)

Directions

  1. Dice up all of your vegetables to your desired chunkiness.
  2. Heat up a pan on medium heat.
  3. Add oil to the pan once any water has evaporated.
  4. Put in the bell pepper and onion, adding garlic later if desired.
  5. Add the tomatoes and butter to the pan once the onions start turning transparent.
  6. Season the vegetables to taste, keeping in mind that the eggs have yet to be added.
  7. Crack the eggs into little wells in the sauce, covering with a lid to steam.
  8. Enjoy!
